About Us

Johny's
Fried Chicken

A fried chicken haven nestled in the heart of Lucena City. We've been serving up mouthwatering, crispy fried chicken for number of years, using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients. Our commitment to quality and flavor is evident in every bite. Come experience the taste of the Bigger, Crispier, and Juicier right here in Lucena City.

History

Our journey began with a diverse range of offerings, including burgers, milk tea, and coffee. It wasn't long before we decided to explore a new culinary adventure: fried chicken. Starting small, we poured our passion into perfecting our recipe, and as our popularity grew, so did our menu. We introduced a variety of exciting flavors to delight our customers, and we also expanded our coffee selection, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for everyone to enjoy.

Follow us on our social media platforms for the latest updates, delicious recipes, and foodie news!
